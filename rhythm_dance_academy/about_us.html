A Place For Those Who Love To Dance!

Here, passion meets rhythm, and creativity flows with every beat. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or just starting your journey, this is where you can express yourself, connect with fellow dance enthusiasts, and let the music inspire your soul. Step into a world where every move tells a story, and every dancer belongs.

Radhika Sonar - Principal

I started dancing since i was 3 years old at India before moving to Perth. Dancing is the only thing i can do my whole life. I grew up doing dance and participating in different dance competitions before becoming a dance teacher. Dancing has always been my passion and now I’m extremely lucky to get to do what I love everyday as my job. Now all I want to do is share the love and excitement that dance brings with everyone I can. I pride myself on creating a space where everyone feels welcome and has a chance to dance because there is nothing better to do than dance!

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals of all ages and skill levels to express themselves through the art of dance. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive environment where students can cultivate their passion, build confidence, and achieve their full potential. Through high-quality instruction, creativity, and community, we strive to nurture the physical, emotional, and artistic growth of every dancer, fostering a lifelong love for movement and performance.

Our History

Founded with a passion for dance and a vision to create a vibrant community, our dance school has been a place where individuals come together to learn, grow, and celebrate the art of movement. From humble beginnings, we have evolved into a leading institution, offering diverse dance styles and fostering creativity in dancers of all ages. Our history is rooted in a deep love for the craft, with a commitment to excellence in both training and performance. Over the years, we have built a strong community of dancers, instructors, and supporters, all united by the shared joy of dance. Today, we continue to inspire and guide the next generation of dancers while cherishing the rich legacy we have created.
